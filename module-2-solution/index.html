Pizza Recepies

Step 1

Mix 200 ml warm water with 1 tablespoon yeast in a bow. Then add 2 and a half cups of flour with a pinch of salt and sugar and a tablespoon of olive oil. Mix everything until it is smooth for about 10 minutes. Cover it and leave it to rest for 4 hours.

Step 2

Preheat oven and place baking paper on a tray. Create a crust along the edge of the dough and then spread it out until it has the desired width. Add tomato sause on the top and then add mozarella. Sprinkle with basil and add any topings of you desire.

Step 3

Bake the pizza until the crust is golden and the cheese has melted. Take it out and enjoy while still hot!
